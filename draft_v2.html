Why VinoTrust?

As Australia’s reputation as a wine producer strengthens, so does the risk of wine fraud. Through deceptive practices the reputation of wine producers in all major wine producing countries is being threatened.

VinoTrust aims to assure authenticity making your data immutable and verifiable through blockchain. With VinoTrust, customers can be assured of your wines’ authenticity, provenance and integrity.

Conceived and designed by wine industry people and developed by leaders in digital and blockchain technology, this is a distinctly ‘out of industry’ innovation.

Why blockchain?

A blockchain solution is not just about technology, it’s about solving problems that have been insolvable before due to the inability of the ecosystem to share information in a transparent, immutable and trusted manner. Unlike other systems of record, blockchain technology provides a trusted record of data.

How VinoTrust works

VinoTrust provides the foundation for a new generation of transactions that establishes trust and transparency. The software-as-a-service (SaaS) solution is working to ensure that trust enables, rather than hinders.

VinoTrust certified

Each bottle of wine has a recorded history, permanently recorded in blockchain. We can provide verified history for each unit.

Retail and consumer verified

When the consumer purchases a bottle of wine they are able to verify its authenticity. They can be assured the wine they receive is genuine.

Compliance and integration

Simplify regulatory compliance such as LIP. Integration with current management systems' API.

We can verify

Each stage of the supply chain is verified. Supply chain becomes transparent to the extent that we want it to be.
