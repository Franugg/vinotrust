Blockchain based supply chain solution for the wine industry

Why VinoTrust?

Through deceptive practices the global reputation of wine authenticity is being threatened. VinoTrust will assure this authenticity by making your data immutable and verifiable through blockchain. Conceived and designed by wine industry people and developed by leaders in digital and blockchain technology, this is a distinctly ‘out of Industry’ solution.

Authenticity

Combats deceptive packaging, copied labels, refilled bottles and misrepresentation of variety and region.

Streamline

Streamlines wine production supply chain management.

Global reputation

Enhance and protect your global reputation.

Why blockchain?

A blockchain solution is not just about technology, it’s about solving problems that have been insolvable before due to the inability of the ecosystem to share information in a transparent, immutable and trusted manner. Unlike other systems of record, blockchain technology provides a trusted record of data.

How VinoTrust works

VinoTrust provides the foundation for a new generation of transactions that establishes trust and transparency. The software-as-a-service (SaaS) solution is working to ensure that trust enables, rather than hinders.

VinoTrust certified

The provenance of each bottle of wine is permanently recorded in Blockchain. A VinoTrust verified history for each unit. A system that helps avoid fraud helps everyone, wine producers, resellers, consumers.

Retail and consumer verified

Reseller reputation enhanced. Consumer confidence reinforced.

Compliance and integration

Streamline record keeping and simplify LIP regulatory compliance.

We can verify

Each stage of the supply chain is verified. Supply chain becomes transparent to the extent that we want it to be.
